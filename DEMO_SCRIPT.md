# Meemaw Assist — Demo Script & Pitch Notes

Target length: **3 minutes**. Trim aggressively if the judges give you 2.

---

## The 3-minute pitch structure

| Time | Slide | What you say |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 0:00–0:20 | 1. Title | Hook + who you are |
| 0:20–0:50 | 2. Problem | The human problem |
| 0:50–1:15 | 3. Meet Meemaw | The insight |
| 1:15–2:15 | 4. Demo | **LIVE — this is the whole pitch** |
| 2:15–2:30 | 5. How it works | Quick tech flow |
| 2:30–2:45 | 6. Why we win | Differentiators |
| 2:45–3:00 | 8. Vision | North star quote, thanks |

Skip slide 7 (tech stack detail) unless a judge asks — your README covers it.

## Word-for-word opener (memorize this)

> "Hi, I'm [Name] and this is [Name].
>
> Last week my grandmother called me because her WhatsApp had a red dot she didn't recognize. I was on a train. She felt embarrassed. I felt useless.
>
> Millions of people have this exact moment every single day. The help they Google is written for engineers. The help buttons open chatbots that talk like robots.
>
> So we built Meemaw Assist. Let me show you."

Then tap the button, and just let the demo breathe.

## The live demo — exact steps

**Scenario: "My WiFi isn't working."**

Rehearse this at least 3 times before stage time.

1. Hold up your phone. Say: "Imagine I'm my grandmother. I pick up the phone."
2. Open the Vercel URL in Safari/Chrome (pre-opened tab — don't type on stage).
3. Tap the big blue button. Say out loud: **"My internet is not working."**
4. Wait for Meemaw to respond. She should ask one simple diagnostic question (e.g. "Let's see. Can you see the little black box with lights on it?").
5. Respond: **"Yes, I can see it."**
6. Meemaw will guide the next step.
7. **STOP THE DEMO after 2 exchanges.** You have a pitch to finish. Say: "And she just keeps going, one step at a time, until the internet is back."

### Backup plan — if the mic fails on stage

- Pre-record a 30-second screen recording of a perfect interaction on your laptop.
- Keep it in a video player, one alt-tab away.
- If mic breaks: "The venue WiFi is being dramatic — here's what it looks like on my home network."

**Never fumble on stage. Always have a video.**

## Multilingual bonus moment

If time permits (or a judge asks "what languages"): say one full sentence **in your mother tongue** to Meemaw. Whisper will auto-detect and she'll respond in that language. Judges love this and it's 10 seconds.

## Answers to the questions you will definitely get

### "Isn't this just ChatGPT with voice?"
> "ChatGPT would say 'try restarting your router and checking DNS settings.' Meemaw says, 'Let's try one thing. Can you see the box with lights?' The difference is five hours of prompt engineering and a lot of empathy. Voice is the delivery, persona is the product."

### "How is this different from Siri / Google Assistant?"
> "Siri answers questions. Meemaw solves problems. Siri gives you a list. Meemaw walks beside you, one step at a time, in the language you speak at home."

### "Why not mobile-native?"
> "A PWA works for everyone in 10 seconds. Installing an app is the first tech-support call she was already going to make."

### "What's the business model?"
> "Carriers (like AT&T, the sponsor) pay us per resolved call. Every Meemaw interaction is one fewer call to their support line. Today the industry pays €6-8 per support call. We're cheaper."

### "What's next?"
> "Three things. One: camera input — point the phone at the blinking router, we diagnose visually. Two: screen-share mode for software issues. Three: 'call my grandchild' escalation — if Meemaw can't fix it, she texts them a summary of what's wrong so the human call is 30 seconds instead of 30 minutes."

## Checklist — the night before the pitch

- [ ] Deployed URL is live and works on *your* phone on cellular data (not just WiFi)
- [ ] QR code for the URL printed and taped to your table
- [ ] Backup video recorded and accessible in one tap
- [ ] Phone volume is all the way up (speaker demo needs to be heard from 5 meters)
- [ ] Phone in airplane mode + re-enable WiFi only — prevents texts/notifications during demo
- [ ] Phone battery > 80%
- [ ] Pitch deck on your laptop AND uploaded to a backup URL (Google Drive, etc.)
- [ ] Both teammates know the whole script (in case one of you freezes)
- [ ] Rehearse pitch in front of someone not on your team — watch their face
